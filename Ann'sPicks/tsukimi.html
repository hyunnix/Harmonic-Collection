Tsukimi

Introduction: Located in the heart of the East Village, Tsukimi offers a modern take on kaiseki, a multi-course Japanese dinner. The name Tsukimi translates to moon viewing and refers to the mid-autumn harvest festival honoring the moon at its fullest and brightest. While we partake in the harvest of the earth, we celebrate contemplation, gratitude, and togetherness.

Open hour: Wednesday - Sunday 17:00 - 21:00

Address: 228 E 10th St, New York, NY 10003

Website: https://www.tsukimi.nyc
