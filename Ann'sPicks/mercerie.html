La Mercerie

Introduction: La Mercerie Restaurant lives inside of Roman and Williams Guild in Soho, both created and owned by Stephen Alesch & Robin Standefer, with cuisine by Chef Marie-Aude Rose that celebrates the delights of everyday French cooking. At La Mercerie, Chef Marie-Aude Rose balances her classical training and avant-garde practices in a thoughtful reimagining of French cuisine. Enjoy three different menus with new seasonal dishes and popular La Mercerie classics.

Open hour: Monday - Friday 11:30 - 22:00, Saturday - Sunday 10:00 - 15:00 & 17:00 - 22:00

Address: 53 Howard St, New York, NY 10013

Website: lamercerieny.com
