Ariari

Introduction: Nestled in New York’s East Village, Ariari is a Korean restaurant inspired by the port city of Busan, known for its fresh seafood and comforting Korean dishes. Ariari is a native Korean word that means 'Pave your own way.' We’re proud to be paving our own track, introducing our unique interpretation of Korean gastronomy, especially of Busan's, to New York. We hope to delight those new to the culture and spark feelings of nostalgic joy for those who are familiar.

Open hour: Sunday - Thursday 17:00 - 21:45, Friday - Saturday 17:00 - 22:45
